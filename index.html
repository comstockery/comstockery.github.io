Mantronix - King of the Beats (1988)

The siren heard throughout Donuts comes from Mantronix's King of the Beats. Stereogum published a longer history of "King of the Beats" in 2019.

J Dilla used the Mantronix siren as his producer's tag—a short sound used as a producer's signature. J Dilla first used the Mantronix siren on "Let's Take it Back", a track on his 2003 EP Ruff Draft.

The Mantronix siren appears on 9 of the 31 tracks on Donuts. "King of the Beats" itself contains 12 known samples.
